About Us

Dr. Rajan Arya

Dr. Rajan Arya is a highly esteemed practitioner in the field of Ayurveda, with a profound commitment to restoring health through natural remedies and holistic care. Holding a Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS), along with qualifications in Family Medicine (FMC) and a Post Graduate Diploma in Emergency Medical Services (PGDEMS), Dr. Arya combines traditional Ayurvedic principles with modern medical expertise. With years of experience, he has successfully treated numerous patients, offering personalized care and comprehensive wellness solutions. Driven by a passion for healing and a deep understanding of Ayurvedic philosophy, Dr. Arya continues to positively impact lives through his practice.

Dr. Ayushi Bhardwaj

Dr. Ayushi, a skilled Ayurvedic practitioner with expertise in BAMS, PGDCC, and D-Panchkarma, blends traditional wisdom with modern clinical insights. Specializing in clinical cardiology and Panchkarma therapies, she offers personalized health solutions rooted in Ayurvedic principles. Dr. Ayushi emphasizes holistic care through natural remedies, lifestyle adjustments, and therapeutic techniques. Her compassionate approach and patient-centered philosophy empower individuals to make informed health choices. Dedicated to restoring balance and vitality, she guides her patients on a path to wellness, integrating herbal medicines and dietary modifications for comprehensive health management.
